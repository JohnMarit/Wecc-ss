WEC-SS Has Broad Objectives in The Community.

WEC-SS focuses its programming on needs and vulnerabilities through gender mainstreaming approaches. The programs focus are as follows;

Food Security, livelihood and Nutrition

We are dedicated to improving Food Security, Livelihood, and Nutrition in vulnerable communities. By providing access to nutritious food, promoting sustainable farming practices etc.

Protection (GBV, Child protections, SBC & PSEA)

We are working in Protection focuses on safeguarding vulnerable populations, including addressing Gender-Based Violence (GBV), Child Protection etc.

WASH and Environmental protection(Climate change

We are dedicated to improving Water, Sanitation, and Hygiene (WASH) standards while promoting environmental protection and climate change resilience.

Type and Nature of Organization.

Women Empowerment Centre South Sudan is a National Non-Governmental, Non profit, Non-sectarian and Non-partisan organization working for social development and empowerment of vulnerable women and youth in the Republic of South Sudan.

The organization specializes in delivery of peace dividends including;

  • Transitional justice.
  • Food Security & Nutrition.
  • Protection & Emergency Response.
  • WASH and Health.
  • Education & Capacity building.
  • Gender-balance.

Structure and Departments in Women Empowerment Center South Sudan.

WECSS has Board of Directors headed by it chairperson and the management level is headed by Executive Director who is the formal head of the organization. It has two main department consist of Operation and Support departments.

Gender justice and economic Empowerment

We aim to ensure equal opportunities for all genders by addressing inequalities and empowering individuals, particularly women, to participate fully in the economy.

Food Security and Nutrition

We ensure that everyone has reliable access to sufficient and nutritious food for a healthy life. Together, they aim to eliminate hunger and malnutrition while promoting well-being and sustainable development.

Peace building and Human Rights

We aim to create sustainable peace by addressing conflict roots and promoting individual rights. This approach emphasizes social justice and empowers marginalized communities for lasting harmony.

WASH and Health(SRHH)

WASH and Health (SRHH) combine water, sanitation, and hygiene with sexual and reproductive health to improve health outcomes. This integration is essential for promoting safe reproductive health and reducing disease transmission.

Protection and emergency response

Protection and Emergency Response ensure the safety and support of vulnerable populations during crises, providing essential services and addressing protection needs to aid recovery and build community resilience.

Education and Capacity development

Education and Capacity Development aim to enhance knowledge and skills, empowering individuals and communities for sustainable growth. This approach focuses on building local capabilities through training and resources to foster self-reliance and resilience.
